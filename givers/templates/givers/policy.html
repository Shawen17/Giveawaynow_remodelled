Our mission is to bring people who are willing to give out items they no longer use and people who will find these items valuable to a platform where this can be achieved seamlessly. In doing this, we will require our users to voluntarily provide some of their personal data which will be needed to communicate and enhance item delivery.

The data we collect are through the process of user Signup or if login is through google social login, the data will be collected in the process of the mandatory user account update and when a user wants to give out and pickup an item. The data that will be collected during these processes include the following: Fullname,username,email address,mobile number,state of residence,contact address,gender and profile picture(optional).

Useage of Collected Data

Since the users of this app won't be bots, it is important we know our users by their names, for every internet based service, email address is needed to verify that we are dealing with a real person and it is a means of having a unique identity and also a communication medium to tell our users about the latest happenings, offers and reply to any of their request when they contact us. When a user offer an item or wants to pick up an item on the app, the phone number is needed so that the picking up and delivery of of these items need the dispatch or logistics company to communicate in real time with our users. As it is with the use of the user phone number, so it is with the data of the contact/delivery address of our users to know the location for the dispatch/logistics company to pickup or deliver the relevant items.

Data such as profile picture are not mandatory but when provided, they might be used solely for purpose of appreciation of the givers on this app if they chose not to be anonymous.

Data Sharing

As stated earlier, the data we collect are securely stored with our cloud host and only shared with the particular logistics company who we engage to carry out the obligation of picking up the offered items and delivering requested items and the user data we require is needed to facilitate this process. Apart from this stated reason, on no account would we divulge the data of our users to any third party. For any further clarification, kindly Contact us.

Payment Related Data

For now, our only acceptable means of payment is through Paystack a well established,truthworthy and most of all secure online payment platform. With them, our users can be rest assured that their banking details is safe and secure.
